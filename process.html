My Working Process

Every project follows a clear, structured path — from your first idea to a fully operational virtual studio or broadcast system.

1. Consultation

Initial meeting to understand your vision, technical needs, and site conditions.

2. Planning

System design, equipment selection, and proposal preparation based on your budget and goals.

3. Integration

Hardware setup, software configuration, and workflow testing at your site.

4. Delivery & Training

Handover of system documents and hands-on training for your team.

5. Support & Upgrade

Ongoing maintenance, remote support, and optional yearly upgrade service.
